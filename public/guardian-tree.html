GUARDIAN TREE

Installation - 2023

Guardian Tree is an interactive installation about the sacred trees that are believed to embody spirits and protect small villages since ancient times in South Korea. Some of these ancient trees are registered as natural monuments and their DNAs are preserved by the Cultural Heritage Administration so that they can be cloned in order to continue their lineage. The work can be seen as a digital clone of these animistic trees which interacts with the spinning top. As an ancient toy, the action of spinning the top becomes a ritualistic play.
